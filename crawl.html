INSTRUCTIONS

CONTROLS

By default, the numpad is used to move - you can move up, down, left, right, or diagonally. Press numpad5 to wait. use Q and W to rotate your weapon clockwise or counter-clockwise.

CRAWLING

Use your oil flask to fuel your lantern so you can see.

Fight monsters by navigating your weapon onto them. If there is room, you will knock them backwards and may stun them. Stunned enemies appear in lower case and take double damage. Many weapons deal different damage and stun depending on the type of strike - a SWING is when you hit an enemy by rotating your weapon. a JAB is when you advance on an enemy, thrusting your weapon into them.

Striking an enemy costs STAMINA. Try not to run out! Enemies can be blocked by placing your weapon in their way, but doing so causes them to damage your stamina. If you run out, they will knock your weapon aside and advance on you. (some enemies can do this even if your stamina is full... watch out!) Take a turn off to regain stamina by pressing '5'. Stamina gain is affected by your hunger level.

Luck can be used to undo an action by pressing BACKSPACE. this is a valuable resource! Use luck to undo a mistake, or to "reroll" an action with undesirable results (such as an attack that causes your weapon to break).

items are represented by asterisks and can be collected by walking over them. Dead bodies and chests can be looted by pushing against them (or by smashing them). Items in your inventory can be used by pressing the numeral key corresponding to their slot (on the number bar, not the numpad). Items can be dropped by pressing the D key before pressing the corresponding number. Your tenth slot is currently inacessible, because there is no 10 key on the keyboard. Woops.

OUTSIDE THE DUNGEON

As days pass, luck and health will regenerate and nourishment will deplete. You may REST to pass a day without venturing into the dungeon.

resources can be gained by finding treasure in the dungeon and selling it in town. Make sure to keep your lantern full of oil and your stomach full of food, as a top priority.

when in town, choose a map from the map select dropdown to venture off. Currently, the only dungeon is labeled "cave".

click 'new save' to begin
