Sundon

Adventuring supplies - 
    Older woman with nasty scars from dire wolves and a missing arm
    used to be a loyal treasurehunter for Lord Sundon, but lost her fingers to a dire wolf.
    He has the magicks to restore fingers, she figured he would want to spend it to restore his most valuable treasurefinder to full strength.
    He did not. Magic is his most valuable resource. It's what keeps him alive. Treasurefinders are merely a commodity. 
    Can't treasurefind any more, can't work the fields either without any fingers. Now have to work as a begrudging middleman for local treasurefinders.
    Will give useful information for invading Keep Sundon
